# Elfurat Spices Master Media Guide

This document combines the instructions for image usage and product image specifications, and provides a complete inventory of all available media assets to be used in the website build.

## Agent Guidelines for Website Construction

**IMPORTANT:** The goal is to create a visually rich and immersive experience for Elfurat Spices. 
- **Use ALL provided images** throughout the website to showcase the premium quality, sourcing, and variety of the products. 
- The specific placement of these images (which page, which section) is left to your creative discretion, but ensure that the sheer volume of high-quality assets is utilized to elevate the design. 
- The images provided below cover everything from hero backgrounds, farm sourcing, processing, packaging, export readiness, specific product close-ups, aromatics, dried fruits, nuts, makhana, warehouse infrastructure, certifications, and brand logos. 
- Leverage these visuals to tell the story of a premium, authentic, and globally-ready spice exporter.

## Product Image Specifications

For any product-specific imagery, please adhere to these visual guidelines:
- **Aspect ratio:** 4:5
- **Camera angle:** Consistent 45-degree front-top angle
- **Lighting:** Soft diffused daylight-style studio lighting
- **Background:** Clean warm neutral light beige matte surface
- **Style:** Simple, elegant, realistic, premium e-commerce catalog look
